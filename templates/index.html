About BEARDUK

Categories: Band

Service Area: Hampshire

BEARD is a high-energy live band bringing beard-level vibes to pubs, breweries, and venues across the South of England. Known for their infectious stage presence and tongue-in-cheek "beardy" branding, the group have carved out a loyal following through regular gigs at local favourites like The Old George in Fair Oak, The Anglers in Bishopstoke, and Steamtown Brewery in Eastleigh.

Blending rock, indie, and pub-friendly anthems with their own spin, BEARD are as much about fun as they are about music. Their shows are packed with sing-along covers, original material, and the kind of atmosphere that makes everyone feel like part of the band. Whether it's a packed brewery taproom or a village pub, they specialise in turning an ordinary night into a beard-powered party.
